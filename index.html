Stop Yapping.

Let's get to the point.

Imagine you're in a conversation, and someone starts with:

❌ Don't do this

  • "Skibidi toilet rizz sigma Ohio W remix 💀💀💀"
  • "Bro I'm literally him fr 😤🔥"
  • "NPC moment ong no cap"
  • "Can I interest you in a Minecraft YouTuber lore deep dive?"

It's confusing and unproductive. Instead, try being direct:

✅ Try this instead

  • "Hey, do you have a moment to discuss our project?"
  • "I have a question about the meeting schedule."
  • "I'm feeling bored. Want to play a game?"
  • "Can we catch up later today?"

Being clear and concise helps everyone communicate better.
