Stop Yapping.

Say what you mean. Respect everyone's time.

Don’t do this:

  • “Hey, you there?”
  • “I was just wondering if maybe you could possibly help me with something real quick...”
  • “Before I start, let me give you a bit of context…”

Do this instead:

  • “Hi, can you help me reset my password?”
  • “I need your input on the sales deck by 3 PM.”
  • “There’s a bug in the login flow—can you check it?”
